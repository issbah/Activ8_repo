"Handy Portion Sizes"

Remember!

Choose fresh over processed whenever possible.

Limit sodium, saturated fats, and added sugars.

Desserts are not typically nutritious, however, if your body craves a flavor/food that may be classified as dessert, keep moderation/portion sizes in mind.

In general, try to drink between half an ounce and an ounce of water for every pound of your bodyweight each day.

How many servings you require for each food/ nutrient group depends on your personal needs. (Refer to the link in the Resources section below to learn more.)
