WHY IT MATTERS:

When it comes to getting the best results, knowing what type of body type you have can make a big difference. Understanding your body type will help you fine-tune both training and nutrition to focus on what works best for you and maximize your progress.

BACKGROUND:

There are mainly three different body types → Ectomorph, Mesomorph and Endomorph. Starting with Ectomorph, they are naturally skinny and gaining weight for them can become hard and they also seem to have a “fast metabolism”. However, eating the correct foods will help them reach their goals faster. Second, Mesomorphs are really athletic looking and their metabolism works like a charm. For this body type, fitness comes naturally but can be difficult to maintain, so attaching themselves to healthy habits is a great solution for them. Lastly, Endomorphs are naturally bigger and losing fat for them can be a struggle but with the right amount of training and nutrition, anything is possible.
